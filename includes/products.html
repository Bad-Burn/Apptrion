Our Products

Innovative solutions designed for real-world impact

Media Vault

A marketplace for original photos, illustrations, music, and sound effects, empowering independent artists to monetize their work while giving buyers flexible, culturally relevant assets.

Key Modules

  • User Module: account creation, profile management, subscription handling
  • Creator Module: upload and manage assets, licensing and pricing controls
  • Marketplace: asset catalog, search and filtering, secure payment gateway

PolishedPro

An all-in-one business management system that bridges the gap between local nail artists and their clients with structured booking, replacing disorganized social media DMs.

Key Features

  • Artist Dashboard: calendar management, earnings tracker, client history, analytics
  • AI Insights: smart scheduling suggestions, revenue forecasting, trend analysis
  • Client Booking Portal: service menu, instant booking, AI assistant chat

LearnLink

Centralizes webinars with details and registration links, allowing instructors to host on their platforms while simplifying discovery and streamlining professional development for learners.

Key Modules

  • User Module: account creation, profile management, event registration
  • Instructor Module: event creation, platform integration, analytics tracking
  • Event Catalog: centralized listing, search and filtering, category organization
